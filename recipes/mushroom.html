Mushroom Recipe

Description

This pattern describes how to crochet a small mushroom.

(Pattern by CutiepieCrochet.)

Ingredients

  • Worsted weight yarn in Aran, Red, White
  • Polyester Fiber Fill
  • 3.75mm crochet hook
  • Yarn needle
  • Scissors

Steps

Stem (in Aran)

  1. mr 6 [6]
  2. inc x6 [12]
  3. BLO, sc around [12]
  4. sc around (12)
  5. sc around (12)

Cap (in Red)

  1. mr 6 [6]
  2. inc x6 [12]
  3. (sc 1, inc)x6 [18]
  4. sc 1, inc (sc 2, inc) x5, sc 1 [24]
  5. sc around [24]
  6. sc around [24]
  7. BLO (sc 2, dec)x6 [18]
  8. (sc 1, dec)x6 [12]

Stuff and embroider spots.
